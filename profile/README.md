# Intersport Rent

Welcome to the official GitHub organization of **Intersport Rent Austria**.  
Here we design, develop, and maintain digital solutions that power our rental ecosystem — from public APIs to web platforms — helping people explore the mountains and enjoy every moment outdoors.

---

### About
**Intersport Rent Austria** is part of the global Intersport network, bringing together passion for sports and innovation.  
We focus on improving digital experiences for customers and partners across Austria and beyond.

---

### Our Mission
To make renting sports equipment simple, smart, and sustainable —  
while delivering technology that connects people with the spirit of adventure.
